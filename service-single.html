Understanding bed bug infestations

Bed bugs hide in mattresses, bed frames, sofas, curtains, electrical sockets, and wall cracks. They multiply quickly and are difficult to eliminate without professional treatment. Early detection and expert intervention are crucial to avoid widespread infestation.
